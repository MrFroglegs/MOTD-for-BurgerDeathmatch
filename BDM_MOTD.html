How To Play

What is 'Fight to Survive'?

Fight To Survive is a combination of endless fast-paced deathmatch and King of the Hill.

    Deathmatching:

    The core element of this gamemode.
  • Kill other players to earn EXP using a variety of weapons at your disposal.
  • Performing multi-kills and killing sprees will earn you bonus EXP.
  • You can carry up to 50 Kg of equipment. The heavier your loadout, the slower your mobility.
  • You can pick up dropped weapons and ammo pouches by pressing E.
  • You can create or join a team at any time. Your teammate's location and current HP will appear on your HUD. See the Controls Section below.

    • King of the Hill point capture:

    The secondary element of this gamemode.
  • Hold down Control Points to become the King. As long as you remain on the point, you will earn EXP, replenish ammunition and 3 Health Points every second to a maximum of 300 HP.
  • Stepping out of the point that you control leaves it open for other players to steal. You will also stop earning the bonuses listed above untill you return to the point.
  • If another player takes control of the point you were holding, they will become King and any bonus HP you have earned will go to them.
  • Control points change every 5 minutes. To remain King, head to the next point before another player takes control.

Controls

    Menus:

  • Tab: View Scoreboard
  • F1: MOTD
  • F3: Team Menu
  • F4: Loadout Menu

    • Weapons:

  • Left Click: Fire.
  • Right Click: Ironsights / Scopes. - Slightly improves accuracy for most weapons.
  • Mousewheel While Zoomed: Adjust zoom.
  • R Key: Reload.
  • C Key: View weapon stats.
  • E Key + Left Click: Quick-Throw Grenade.
  • E Key + Right Click: Melee.
  • Drop Weapon: Bind a key to "dropweapon" in console. Example: bind (any key) dropweapon

    Parkour System:

  • Spacebar: Jump. The faster your momentum, the more jumps you can perform.
  • Spacebar + CTRL Key: Super jump. The more you look up, the higher you'll jump.
  • Holding CTRL in MID-AIR: Glide. Can last up to 5 seconds depending on momentum.
  • Divebomb Attack: While in mid-air, look downward and hold the CTRL Key until you land. Reduces fall damage and damages other players.

    Wall Climbing:

  • Attach to Wall: While in air and directly facing a wall, hold the Spacebar to climb. Lasts 3 seconds before detaching.
  • Detach from Wall: Release Spacebar or press CTRL Key. Pressing CTRL Key makes it easier to go through windows.
